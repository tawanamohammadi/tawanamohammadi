About Me

I am Tawana Mohammadi (توانا محمدی) — an independent AI researcher, educator, and data strategist working at the intersection of ethics, intelligence, and data governance.

My mission is to design human-centered, transparent, and responsible AI systems that empower individuals and organizations through ethical innovation and data literacy.

With over a decade of experience in AI systems, cybersecurity, and education, I explore how algorithms and human values can coexist in transparent and ethical digital ecosystems.

I believe that true progress in AI lies not in power, but in empathy, accessibility, and accountability. Through responsible design and education, I aim to make AI a bridge, not a barrier.
