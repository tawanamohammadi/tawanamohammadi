About Me

I am Tawana Mohammadi (توانا محمدی), an independent AI researcher, educator, and data strategist. My mission is to design human-centered, transparent, and responsible AI systems that empower individuals and organizations through ethical innovation and data literacy.

With over a decade of experience in AI systems, cybersecurity, and education, I explore how algorithms and human values can coexist in transparent and ethical digital ecosystems. I believe that true progress in AI lies not in power, but in empathy, accessibility, and accountability.

"Where ethics and intelligence meet."

My Vision

AI should remain transparent, inclusive, and grounded in empathy. Through responsible design and education, I aim to make AI a bridge, not a barrier, between humans and technology—advancing a world where ethics, transparency, and human dignity define intelligence.
